⚡️KEY FEATURES⚡️

SLAM DUNK

This area features one full-height basketball ring, as well as a lower ring for beginners above runway trampolines surrounded by padded walls and floor mats.
It’s like borrowing the legs of the world’s best basket-baller.

Get creative and go for style points. It is one of our most addictive attractions – give it a try to find out why.

BIG BAG

This HUGE bag provides a massive soft landing spot for your aerial endeavors.
It’s also a great place to work on moves that you can take to the High Performance trampolines, the street or the slopes.
Lying at the end of rebound tumble tracks, the giant inflatable bag sits in a five-foot-deep pit.

Go Wild but be safe!

HIGH PERFORMANCE AREA

High performance trampolines enable greater bounce and height, perfect for skills development and more advanced manoeuvres.﻿
They are often used by athletes pursuing advanced performance around sports such skiing, snowboarding, wake-boarding and skateboarding.
It’s also a training ground for free-runners and anyone above 110cm involved in dedicated trampoline sports.

Take a look at these awesome tricks you can do at this area!

FREE-JUMP ARENA

The Free-Jump Arena is a giant network of interconnected floor and wall trampolines.
It’s like Free-Running and Parkour made easy!

This is our main stage – with around 28 interconnected trampolines, tumble tracks and banked walls.
Enjoy freestyle freedom or just run wild in this oversized playground.

X-PARK: ADVENTURE CHALLENGE COURSE

Developed over a 12-month period in collaboration with the creators of the world’s best parkour and bouldering courses,
X-Park is an Adventure Challenge Course for adrenaline hunters above 125cm.
It combines obstacles & features that can be explored as stand-alone challenges or navigated as one circuit with different levels of difficulty to allow for personal progression and creative expression.

Fans of the “Ninja Warrior” phenomenon will recognise the impressive warped wall, which requires a combination of speed, strength and timing to master.

RUN THE WALL

The Wall combines high performance trampolines with vertical walls of various heights for freestyle tricks and Wall Running.
This area is like a skate park made out of high performance trampolines.

You need to experience Wall Running to believe how much fun and addictive it is.
It’s a super simple concept – use the bounce and rebound properties of high performance trampolines to run up walls!

It was pioneered by the Cirque Du Soleil and takes a few tips to master, but once you get it, it’s pure awesomeness every time.
Take a look at how it is done!
